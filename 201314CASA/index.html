Welcome to CASA!

With over 50 years of tradition, we have become the premier Chinese American Organization on UCSD campus. As a Chinese-interest community, we pride ourselves in providing the most thrilling events, lifelong friends, and sense of Chinese culture. We reach out to Chinese American students as well as all other ethnicity groups! We can't wait to meet you at our upcoming events and meetings!
